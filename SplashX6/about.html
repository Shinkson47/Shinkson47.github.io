Splash X6 is a work-in-progress strategy settlement game inspired by Freeciv and unciv. it is the 6TH INSTALMENT IN THE SPLASH PROJECT.

it's a PASSION PROJECT BUILT BY TWO DMU students with the primary goal in learning libgdx, kotlin and java.

originally, splash x6 was to be built with LWJGL. we made a swift transition to libgdx after realising our lack of experience with opengl and openal would slow development, driving us to seek an engine which handled the tricky parts for us, allowing focus on developing the functionally important parts.
